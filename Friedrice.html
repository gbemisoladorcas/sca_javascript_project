Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredient

2 Cups cooked Rice 1 Cup Mixed Vegetables I used Carrots, Sweet Peas, Sweet Corn and Green Beans 1 cup Onion diced 2 Scallions 1 Cup Beef Liver Cubed 1/2 Teaspoon Thyme 1 Teaspoon Curry powder 1/2 cup Chicken stock 1 Stock cube 3 Tablespoons Oil for frying Salt to taste

Add the parboiled Rice to the stock and cook until the water is dried up. Preheat the oil in a Pan, throw in the Onions and fry for a minute or two, add the scallions, mixed vegetables and the seasonings ( Thyme, Curry Powder, Salt and Stock Cube) Throw in the rice and stir-fry for about 3 to 5 Minutes. Take it off the heat and serve
